What it does

Story time, quiz time, celebration time.

1

Kids listen to stories

PIP reads each adventure aloud so children can settle in, follow the words, and enjoy the story world.

2

Understand the story

Short, focused scenes help kids remember characters, places, and what happened next.

3

Answer playful quizzes

After listening, children answer story questions that turn comprehension into a cheerful challenge.

4

Get instant feedback

Right answers feel exciting, and missed answers gently invite another try with PIP beside them.

5

Replay favorite stories

Kids can hear an adventure again, practice the quiz, and build confidence at their own pace.

6

Pick new story worlds

A colorful story selection menu lets children jump into forests, space, oceans, castles, and more.

Story library

Ten themed adventures in one lightweight app.

Every story includes its own visual theme, companion color palette, narrative, and five comprehension questions.
